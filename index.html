Meditation



Meditation can be thought of as a way to train your mind.


The concept comes from the Sanskrit word Bhāvanā (Sanskrit: भावना, also bhāvana)
which literally means "development", cultivating or "producing" in the sense of "calling into existence".


In a more modern translation would problably be something like practice or exercice.


Scientists have found that practicing maeditation regularly can bring
many benefits for you, such as:


  • Stress reduction
  • Stress reduction
  • Anxiety control
  • Emotional health
  • Self-Awareness Enhancement
  • Attention Span Becomes Lengthened
