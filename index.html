Welcome


Our minds are involved in any experience we have.


This can seem quite obvious, but we usually don't realise how much activity happens in our minds every day.


We know that our routines as developers can be a little tricky with many deadlines to follow up or a lot of teamwork that is not always easy to deal with.


The good news is that we can train our minds to experience these daily situations more happily.


That's why we created this page as an easy to access guide for mindfulness life.


Enjoy!
